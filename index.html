About

I study fertility-related distress, focusing on inclusive measurement and theoretical modelling. I combine co-design with lived experience and clinicians, qualitative synthesis, and psychometric validation (EFA/CFA/SEM) to deliver tools that improve clinical care and research comparability.

Research Focus

  • Measurement of fertility-related distress (content validity, readability, invariance)
  • Conceptual domains: emotional, cognitive/rumination, identity/meaning, stigma/social legitimacy, treatment burden, autonomy/power, uncertainty/loss
  • Mixed-methods methodology & open science
